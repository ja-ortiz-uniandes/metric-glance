Price rounding

Prices are rounded up to the next whole amount when they're within the cents threshold below it, and shown with a dotted underline. Hover or tap to see the exact price. Changes apply to pages after they reload.

Number formatting

The extension shows the metric unit that reads most simply: no leading "0.", fewest decimals, then fewest digits. So 0.62 m becomes "62 cm" and 551 ml stays "551 ml" instead of "0.55 L".

Advanced: per-measurement override

Override the choice above for one measurement. Display = shown inline (leave all off to follow the table above). Hover = shown as extra conversions in the hover panel.
